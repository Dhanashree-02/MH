Trek Details

  • Location : Malshej Ghat, Pune
  • Price: 1599
  • The Aadrai Jungle Trek is the best way to explore one of Sahyadri's most beautiful and untouched Jungles. This tour will take you deep into the heart of this lush, green forest, where you can see waterfalls, caves, valleys, shepherd trails, famous.

Itinerary

  • From Pune - 11.00 PM Night journey begins.
  • From Mumbai - 10:00 PM Night journey begins
  • 02.30 AM We Reach the Base village
  • 02.30 AM to 05:00 AM Small Nap
  • 05.00 AM to 06:00 AM Breakfast and Refreshment
  • 06:00 AM Trek begins
  • 08.30 AM Will reach at end of Forest
  • 09:30 AM Start Return Journey to base village
  • 12.00 PM Lunch (inclusive of veg / non-veg)
  • 01.00 PM Return Journey to Pune / Mumbai
  • 06.00 PM In Reach Home with Loads of Memories

(01 - 02 hrs delay is possible depending on the speed of trekkers, traffic on the road & other unavoidable circumstances.. so keep that buffer)

Trek Event Details:

  • Difficulty level - Medium
  • Endurance level medium
  • Region: Malshej Ghat, Ahmednagar
  • Total time required for climbing: Max 03.30 hours one side vai Khireshwar route
  • Age Suitability: 15 to 45 Years

Cost Includes

  • Travelling by Non AC private bus
  • Breakfast
  • Tea / Coffee
  • Lunch
  • Basic First aid
  • Expertise
  • Trek Guide
  • Forest Fee

Things To Carry

  • Torch (optional)
  • Please wear full pants and full sleeves t-shirts to protect from tanning, thorns, insects during the trek
  • Snacks (Energy bars, Chikki, Biscuits, Sprouts, Chips, Dry fruits)
  • Glucon D/Protein shakes/ORS
  • Extra pair of clothes
  • 2/3 litres of water (Mandatory)
  • Any personal medicines
  • Good grip trekking shoes
  • Rains are expected so please carry a raincover / Jacket & cover your phones also in a plastic cover
