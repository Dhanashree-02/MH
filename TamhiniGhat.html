Trek Details

  • Location : PUNE TO MUMBAI
  • Price: 1799
  • Tamhini Ghat features lush greenery, waterfalls, and winding roads, offering stunning Western Ghats views and a serene escape.

Itinerary

  • 5.30:00AM : Pickup time from pune and mumbai
  • 09.00am: Small Breakfast and Start exploring
  • 2.30pm : Descent Lunch
  • 03.00pm : return to the journey
  • 10.30 pm :End of a Beautiful Journey

(01 - 02 hrs delay is possible depending on the speed of trekkers, traffic on the road & other unavoidable circumstances.. so keep that buffer)

Trek Event Details:

  • Height: Approx. 2,200 feet
  • Difficulty: Easy to Moderate
  • Duration: 4 hours
  • Terrain: Winding paths, moderate inclines, and scenic trails
  • Key Attractions: Waterfalls, lush greenery, panoramic Western Ghats views

Cost Includes

  • Travelling
  • Breakfast
  • Lunch
  • Basic First aid
  • Non AC SEATER BUS
  • Forest fees

Things To Carry

  • Torch (optional)
  • Please wear full pants and full sleeves t-shirts to protect from tanning, thorns, insects during the trek
  • Snacks (Energy bars, Chikki, Biscuits, Sprouts, Chips, Dry fruits)
  • Glucon D/Protein shakes/ORS
  • Extra pair of clothes
  • 2/3 litres of water (Mandatory)
  • Any personal medicines
  • Good grip trekking shoes
  • Rains are expected so please carry a raincover / Jacket & cover your phones also in a plastic cover

Pickup Points

  • Pune : JM corner shivajinagar - nashik phata
  • Mumbai : Adhari-sion-thane
