Trek Details

  • Location: PUNE TO Mumbai
  • Price: 1599
  • Ratangad Fort, features breathtaking views of the Sahyadri range, ancient caves, and a unique natural rock formation called "Nedhe." Ideal for adventure seekers and history lovers.

Itinerary

  • 09:00pm : Reporting time
  • 4.30am: starting trek
  • 7.00am :explore the unknown
  • 11.00am :return Journey Of Ratangad Trek
  • 02.00pm : Descend & Lunch
  • 11.00 am :End of a Beautiful Journey

(01 - 02 hrs delay is possible depending on the speed of trekkers, traffic on the road & other unavoidable circumstances, so keep that buffer)

Trek Event Details:

  • Height: 4,255 feet
  • Difficulty: Moderate
  • Duration: 4-5 hours (one way)
  • Terrain: Steep trails, rock patches, and ancient caves
  • Key Attractions: Panoramic views, Nedhe (natural rock hole), historical fortifications

Cost Includes

  • Travelling
  • Breakfast
  • Lunch
  • Forest charges
  • Basic First aid

Things To Carry

  • Torch (optional)
  • Please wear full pants and full sleeves t-shirts to protect from tanning, thorns, insects during the trek
  • Snacks (Energy bars, Chikki, Biscuits, Sprouts, Chips, Dry fruits)
  • Glucon D/Protein shakes/ORS
  • Extra pair of clothes
  • 2/3 litres of water (Mandatory)
  • Any personal medicines
  • Good grip trekking shoes
  • Rains are expected so please carry a raincover / Jacket & cover your phones also in a plastic cover

Pickup Points

  • Pune pickup: JM corner Shivajinagar - Nashik Phata
  • Mumbai pickup: Andheri - sion -thane
